History of Popular Sports

Football (Soccer)

Football, known as soccer in some countries, has ancient roots with versions played in China, Greece, and Rome. The modern game began in England in the 19th century, with standardized rules established in 1863. The Football Association (FA) was formed in England to codify the rules, leading to the global sport we know today.

Basketball

Basketball was invented in 1891 by Dr. James Naismith in Springfield, Massachusetts, USA. He developed the game as an indoor activity to keep his students active during the winter. The sport quickly gained popularity and evolved into the fast-paced game played worldwide today.
